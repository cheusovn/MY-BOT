| Glass fill  | rgba(255,255,255,0.04) | — | Card backgrounds             |

### Glow effect recipe (for any editor):
- Outer glow: #C8FF00, blur 40px, opacity 30–50%
- Text glow: duplicate layer, blur 8px, opacity 60%, blending: Screen

### Font weights used on page:
- Unbounded 800–900 → headings and titles
- Manrope 400–800 → body, labels, badges
- JetBrains Mono 400–700 → numbers, stats, countdown, prices
